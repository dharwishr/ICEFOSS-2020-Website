About ICEFOSS

ICEFOSS is a Free Software Conference organized by FISAT Free Software Cell (FFSC). ICEFOSS 2020, scheduled on February 3,4 2020 includes Workshops covering different aspects of Free Software. ICEFOSS 2020 is the eighth edition of this conference. To know about the past conferences click here.

About FISAT

Federal Institute of Science And Technology (FISAT) was established in 2002 under the aegis of Federal Bank Officers' Association Educational Society (FBOAES). The college has carved a niche for itself in education world, eloquently demonstrated by the flying colors attained by its students in academics, placements as well as in extra curricular and co-curricular activities.

Introduction to Docker and Kubernetes

Abstract

This session will introduce you to the basics of Docker and Kubernetes. Widely used in industry Docker, provides virtualizination capabilities. Docker is used to run software packages called "containers". Originally designed by Google, Kuberenetes is widely used in industry for automating application deployment.

Prerequisite

Basic GNU/Linux commands

Resource Person

  • Midhun Kandoth
  • Johny James

Venue

Central Computing Facility - L4

Machine Learning using TensorFlow 2.0

Abstract

This session will introduce you well into the general concept TensorFlow. TensorFlow 2.0 represents a major milestone in the library’s development. Over the past few years, one of TensorFlow’s main weaknesses, and a big reason many people switched over to PyTorch, was its very complicated API. There will be a hands on experiance with TensorFlow 2.0 and different work loads, so come prepared :).

Prerequisite

Basic GNU/Linux commands

Resource Person

Sreeraj M

Venue

Natural Language Processing

Abstract

This session will introduce you into the basics of Natural Language Processing. Natural Language Processing is the technology used to aid computers to understand the human’s natural language. It’s not an easy task teaching machines to understand how we communicate.

F.A.Q

Q. Will accommodation be provided or not ?
A. As per request , Yes! But there will be a nominal fee for accommodation and dinner
Q. Will I have to pay ₹400 ,if I attend just one day of the programme?
A. Yes, you will have to pay ₹400 even if you attend the event for only 1 day.
Q. How do I reach Fisat via Bus/Train?
A. You can either take the bus or train to arrive at Angamaly. From there are multiple possibilities :
  • Take an auto-rikshaw that will take you to FISAT for about ₹150-180.
  • Take a private bus that goes directly to FISAT.
  • Take a private bus to Mookkannoor and :
    • Walk for 15 minutes to FISAT.
    • Take an auto-rikshaw from Mookkannoor to FISAT for about ₹50.
