What is #MAPMYKOCHI?

FISAT Free Software Cell (FFSC), in collaboration with ICEFOSS 2020, is striving to undertake a coordinated mapping event Mapathon, under the tagline #MAPMYKOCHI. The committee is to implement the project from February 3rd along with the commencing of ICEFOSS 2020 till the end of February.

Why to #MAPMYKOCHI?

The session strives to improve the present open source map where the delegates are invited to make online map improvements in their local area to improve coverage and to help disaster risk assessment and energy management using OpenStreetMap for the same.

About FISAT

Federal Institute of Science And Technology (FISAT) was established in 2002 under the aegis of Federal Bank Officers' Association Educational Society (FBOAES). The college has carved a niche for itself in education world, eloquently demonstrated by the flying colors attained by its students in academics, placements as well as in extra curricular and co-curricular activities.
